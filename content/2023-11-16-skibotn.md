### Aerosol optical depth

> <span style="color:yellow;">Opacity of the stratosphere</span> due to aerosol
> scattering

### Radiative forcing

> Top-of-the-atmosphere <span style="color:yellow;">energy imbalance</span> due to
> volcanic aerosols
