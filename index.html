Download the presentations as PDFs by adding ?print-pdf to the end of a link:
https://slides.eirik.re/2022-02-08-demo.html?print-pdf

You may also include the speaker notes, both during a presentation and when printing, by adding showNotes=separate-page to the URL:
https://slides.eirik.re/2022-02-08-demo.html?showNotes=separate-page&print-pdf (PDF with notes on a separate page.)
https://slides.eirik.re/2022-02-08-demo.html?showNotes=true&print-pdf (PDF with notes overlaid on the presentation slide.)
https://slides.eirik.re/2022-02-08-demo.html?showNotes=separate-page (HTML view with notes on the side. Note that while presenting, pressing the "S" key is the better option.)
